# Module 1: Passive Reconnaissance

## What is Passive Recon?
Passive reconnaissance is gathering information **without** interacting with the target directly. The target has no way of knowing you are looking at them.

## Common Sources:
1. **Google Dorking**: Using advanced search operators to find hidden files.
2. **Social Media**: LinkedIn, Twitter, Facebook (to find employee names, emails, and tech stacks).
3. **WHOIS**: Finding ownership details of a domain.
4. **Shodan**: A search engine for internet-connected devices.

## Why do it?
It is the safest way to start. Since you aren't "touching" the target, you won't trigger any alarms.
