# Operation: Obsidian Shadow

## The Mission
You have been hired by "SecureCorp," a global tech company, to perform a full-scale "Red Team" engagement. Your goal is to simulate a real-world attack to find a critical vulnerability that could lead to the theft of the company's most prized possession: **The Obsidian Key** (a secret file).

## The Rules of Engagement (ROE)
1. **Stay Anonymous**: All attacks must originate from your secure VM.
2. **Do No Harm**: Do not delete files or crash services unless explicitly required for the exploit.
3. **Document Everything**: Every command, screenshot, and finding must be recorded for the final report.
4. **The Goal**: You start with **ZERO** information and **ZERO** credentials.

## The Story
SecureCorp believes their network is impenetrable. They have a public-facing website, a complex internal API, a Windows Active Directory forest, and a secure Cloud storage vault. You are the shadow. Your job is to prove them wrong.
