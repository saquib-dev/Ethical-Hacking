# Module 1: Wireless Security Basics

## How WiFi Works
WiFi sends data through the air using radio waves. Because it's in the air, anyone with the right antenna can "listen" to it.

## Common Encryption:
1. **WEP (Old)**: Extremely weak. Can be cracked in minutes.
2. **WPA2 (Standard)**: Uses a 4-way handshake to connect. The handshake can be captured and cracked offline.
3. **WPA3 (New)**: Much stronger; prevents most offline dictionary attacks.

## Monitor Mode vs Managed Mode
- **Managed Mode**: Your card connects to an AP (Normal use).
- **Monitor Mode**: Your card "listens" to all traffic in the air, even if it's not meant for you. (Required for hacking).
