# Module 1: The Human Element

## What is Social Engineering?
Social Engineering is the art of manipulating people into giving up confidential information or performing actions. It is often easier to "hack a person" than to "hack a computer".

## Common Psychology Tricks:
1. **Urgency**: "Your account will be deleted in 2 hours! Click here now!"
2. **Authority**: "I am the CEO of the company, I need this password for an emergency."
3. **Fear**: "Your computer has a virus! Call this number to fix it."
4. **Curiosity**: "Check out these leaked photos of your co-worker!"
