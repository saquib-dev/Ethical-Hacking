# Module 2: Common Attack Vectors

## Phishing
Sending fake emails that look real to steal passwords or install malware.
- **Spear Phishing**: Targeting a specific person (using their name, job title).
- **Whaling**: Targeting high-level executives (CEOs).

## Pretexting
Creating a fake story (a pretext) to gain trust. 
Example: Pretending to be an IT support worker who needs to "verify" a user's password.

## Baiting
Leaving a physical "bait" (like a USB drive) in a public place. When a curious person plugs it in, the malware installs automatically.
