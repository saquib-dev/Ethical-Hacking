# Lab 2: Crafting a Convincing Email

## Goal: Practice writing a phishing email that bypasses suspicion.

## Task:
Write a mock email for the following scenario:
*You are an IT Administrator. You need users to change their passwords because of a "security breach".*

## Checklist for your email:
- [ ] Use a fake but professional-looking "From" address.
- [ ] Create a sense of urgency.
- [ ] Use a link that *looks* real but goes to your phishing page (e.g., `company-security-update.com` instead of `company.com`).
- [ ] Use a professional signature.

## Success Check:
- [ ] I have written a convincing phishing email.
- [ ] I can identify the psychology tricks (Urgency, Authority) used.
