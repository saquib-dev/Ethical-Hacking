# Module 1: The Law

## The Golden Rule
**NEVER** attack a system you do not have explicit, written permission to test. Even if you "don't do any damage", accessing a system without permission is illegal in almost every country.

## Types of Hackers:
1. **White Hat**: Ethical hackers. They have permission and help fix the holes.
2. **Black Hat**: Malicious hackers. They steal data or cause damage.
3. **Grey Hat**: Somewhere in between. They might find a bug without permission but don't use it for evil. (Still risky and often illegal).

## Safe Places to Practice:
- **TryHackMe**: Guided labs for beginners.
- **Hack The Box**: More advanced machines.
- **VulnHub**: Downloadable VMs to hack locally.
- **Bug Bounty Programs**: Companies that PAY you to find bugs.
