# Module 2: Bug Bounty

## What is Bug Bounty?
A "Bug Bounty" is a program where companies (like Google, Meta, or Apple) invite hackers to find bugs in their systems. If you find a real bug and report it, they pay you money.

## How to start:
1. **Join a Platform**: 
   - **HackerOne**
   - **Bugcrowd**
2. **Read the Scope**: The "Scope" tells you exactly which websites you are allowed to hack. If it's not in the scope, **DON'T TOUCH IT**.
3. **Report Responsibly**: Once you find a bug, you send a private report to the company. Do NOT post it on social media until the company fixes it.

## The Reward
Depending on the bug, you can earn anywhere from $50 to $100,000+.
