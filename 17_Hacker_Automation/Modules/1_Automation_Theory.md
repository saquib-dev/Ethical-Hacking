# Module 1: Why Automation?

## From Tool-User to Tool-Creator
A professional hacker doesn't just use tools; they automate them. Automation allows you to:
1. **Scale**: Scan 10,000 IPs instead of 10.
2. **Customize**: Create a tool that specifically targets a unique bug you found.
3. **Speed**: Perform complex reconnaissance in seconds.

## The Hacker's Toolkit
- **Bash**: Best for chaining existing Linux tools together (e.g., Nmap $\rightarrow$ Grep $\rightarrow$ Nuclei).
- **Python**: The gold standard for exploit development and custom tooling.
- **Go**: Increasingly popular for high-performance tools and cross-platform binaries.
