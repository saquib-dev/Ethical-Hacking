# Module 2: Online vs Offline Attacks

## Offline Attack (Hash Cracking)
You have already stolen the password file (the hashes). You can try millions of passwords per second on your own machine without the target knowing.
- **Tools**: John the Ripper, Hashcat.

## Online Attack (Brute Forcing)
You try to log in to a live service (like a Website or SSH) by guessing passwords.
- **Risk**: The target can see your attempts in their logs and lock your IP.
- **Tools**: Hydra, Medusa.
