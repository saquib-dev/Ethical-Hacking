# Module 2: VPN and Tor (Staying Hidden)

## VPN (Virtual Private Network)
A VPN hides your real IP address by sending your traffic through another server.
- **Pros**: Hides your location from the website you visit.
- **Cons**: The VPN provider can see your traffic.

## Tor (The Onion Router)
Tor bounces your traffic through three different servers (nodes) around the world.
- **Pros**: Extremely hard to track.
- **Cons**: Very slow.

## Golden Rule of Anonymity
**Never** log into your personal accounts (Facebook, Gmail, Bank) while using your anonymity tools. If you do, you link your real identity to your hidden IP.
